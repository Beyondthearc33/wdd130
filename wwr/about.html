History

River Riders was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts who shared a love for adventure and the beauty of nature. Inspired by their own thrilling experiences navigating rivers and exploring untamed wilderness, they sought to create a company that would provide unforgettable rafting expeditions for fellow adventurers.

The founders, comprised of seasoned rafters and outdoor guides, pooled their expertise and resources to establish River Riders as a premier destination for adrenaline-pumping river adventures. With a commitment to safety, professionalism, and environmental stewardship, River Riders quickly gained a reputation for delivering exhilarating experiences while prioritizing the protection of the natural landscapes they explored.

In its early years, River Riders operated from a modest base camp nestled along the banks of a scenic river, offering guided rafting trips to local enthusiasts and tourists seeking outdoor thrills. As word of mouth spread and demand for their adventures grew, the company expanded its operations, adding new routes, acquiring top-of-the-line equipment, and recruiting experienced guides to lead expeditions.

Throughout its journey, River Riders has remained dedicated to its core values of adventure, safety, and environmental responsibility. The company has forged partnerships with conservation organizations, participated in river clean-up initiatives, and implemented sustainable practices to minimize its ecological footprint.

Today, River Riders stands as a leading authority in the world of river rafting, offering a diverse range of expeditions catering to adventurers of all skill levels and interests. From pulse-pounding whitewater rapids to leisurely scenic floats, River Riders continues to inspire thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike, providing unforgettable experiences on the water that will be cherished for a lifetime.
